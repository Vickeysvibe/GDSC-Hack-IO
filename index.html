Rules and Regulations

  • Registration Duration: March 18th to March 21st.
  • Submission Requirement: Participants must submit their ideas through an abstract.
  • Submission Method: Abstracts should be submitted via the provided Google Form.
  • Communication of Further Instructions: Further instructions will be communicated to shortlisted teams via email.
  • Focus Area: Ideas should center around the Internet of Things (IoT) domain or open innovation.
  • Shortlisting Process: Shortlisted teams will be notified via email.
  • Prototype Development: Shortlisted teams must build a working prototype before the offline event.
  • Team Composition: Teams can comprise 2-4 members.
